About

In 2006 I moved to Nashville to attend college. I grew to love the city and decided to stay after graduating. I have always enjoyed helping others and improving their overall quality of life. This desire, along with my curiosity for technology is what drove me to become a software developer, and to seek the help of Nashville Software School in doing so. I have learned more in a few months than I ever could have learned on my own. Growing up, I have always been excited about Science and Technology and was on my high school's exclusive technical path. While in college, my mission was to combine these two passions by studying Biology and adding Bioinformatics. In the midst of this, I found that my true calling laid in developing applications in different technology stacks. Now, one of my ultimate goals in life is to use my technical skills and abilities to create something that can be useful in people's everyday lives.

Projects

Step into My Office

Simo is the web-app for my Front-End capstone at Nashville Software School. Developed mainly with AngularJS, BootStrap and Firebase, this app is used to demonstrate the culmination of my skills in Front-End web development. SiMO is a Notes sharing app intended for high school and college students. You can choose files, add a comment and upload them. There is also a section for adding different classes as well as a real time chat for group study with other students.

Chatty Patty

Chatty is a group project I participated in during my Front-End instruction at Nashville Software School. To complete this project, each of my group members made use of Git and Github for version control, as well as JavaScript, JSON, HTML5 and CSS3. Chatty is a chat application where you can select a user, write a message and delete you message, or all messages as well as change color scheme.

DeadStox

My Back-end capstone, nearing the end of my matriculation through Nashville Software School. Developed using Django, Django Rest and AngularJS. This web app allows you to keep track of your sneaker collection as it continues to grow. As you add sneakers into your closet (database), the app calculates the current resale market value as well as keep a total of the retail value that you actually paid and your potential profit.
